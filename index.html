Sierra Leone National Volunteers

Vision

Is to enhance partnership to contribute to national development through volunteerism .

Mission

Our mission is to work with volunteers to promote rural development in the areas of agriculture, Education, Health and the environment in order to reduce community poverty.

Aims and Objectives

The aim of Sierra Leone National Volunteers is to voluntarily inspire people to contribute to rural development on the basis of volunteerism
